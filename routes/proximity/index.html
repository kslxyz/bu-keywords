Nearness, space, relationship, time, distance, being, the space between people, the space between objects, Proximity is defined as nearness.

Designers think about things and objects and shapes and words, and how they relate to each other in space. Space– full or empty has to do with balance. Maximalism and minimalism both use full and empty to the max. Balance can be an indicator of successful design– having enough media and enough room to breathe. When we think about viewing art we often discuss where our eyes travel. Proximity can be what links visual information. Formally, we can use proximity to create relationships between grouped elements and create more understanding of our subject. It establishes unity and belonging. Nearness can be general. Design is about things on a page, a screen, a book, and how they look together. When I design I'm not thinking about white space and how to fill it. I’m thinking about the elements that go into the composition, and how they can enhance, but not overpower the space.
