[Escape]

How To Never Grow Up:

Rule 1: Don't forget to doodle in the margins of your notebook.

The dictionary definition of escape means to break free from confinement or control.

Rule 2: You don't need an excuse to bake something sweet.

For this project, I chose to focus on how I find mental escape through connecting with my inner child.

Rule 3: I hate to admit it, but mom usually knows best.

These past two years, COVID has forced individuals to find their own ways to escape reality in often creative and untraditional manners - to find joy in simplicity.

Rule 4: Wear pink cowboy boots out to your local bar.

As I grow older, I have come to realize that one does not ever actually have to grow up, and that childhood simplicities can often be found in common places.

Rule 5: Sometimes your laundry belongs in a pile on your bed.

Escape finds me when I call my mom, dress up to go nowhere, watch old films, and sometimes when I just do nothing.

Rule 6: Saturday morning cartoons hit different.

Next time life gets too hard, find freedom through reconnecting with your younger self.

Rule 6: Even better - get ice cream in the dead of winter.

Here is where I find my escape.
