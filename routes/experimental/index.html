Graphic Design = EXPERIMENtAL

Experimentation to mee

Experimentation with processes, projects one decides to pick up, experimenting with software. As a graphic designer having access to the internet allows one to have a literally limitless supply of inspiration and ideas to borrow from for concepts and projects. Experimentation allows one to break from their usual pattern of behavior and open themselves up to that limitless void which in turn supercharges the work that they create. For me the novelty of learning and discovering new things is one of the key aspects that drives my work and my love for graphic design. Getting that aha moment when I figure out how to turn an idea which I have no clue how to translate into what I picture in my head or even better yet when it evolves into something greater is a lovely feeling.
